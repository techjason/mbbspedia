_Sketchy memory palace for Acute Appendicitis_

| No. | Visual Cue | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Worm-shaped pipe at base of tank | Appendix is a true diverticulum of the caecum |
| 2 | Taeniae coli convergence at red X | Appendix base is at convergence of taeniae coli (McBurney’s point) |
| 3 | Foreman in 20s and child apprentice | Peak incidence: 20s-30s in adults; 4-15 in children |
| 4 | Dead End sign on artery | Appendiceal artery is an end-artery; thrombosis leads to gangrene |
| 5 | Stony turd and swollen grape cluster | Etiology: Faecolith (adults) and lymphoid hyperplasia (younger patients) |
| 6 | Pain migration: umbilicus to right pocket | Periumbilical visceral pain (T8-10) migrating to RIF somatic pain |
| 7 | Kicking food away before vomiting | Anorexia typically precedes nausea and vomiting |
| 8 | Pressure on LLQ causing RLQ pain | Rovsing Sign: RIF pain on palpation of LLQ |
| 9 | Leg lift and hip rotation | Psoas sign (retrocaecal) and Obturator sign (pelvic) |
| 10 | MANTRELS scoreboard showing 7 | Alvarado Score ≥ 7 strongly predicts need for surgery |
| 11 | High ladder and pregnancy test | Superior displacement in pregnancy; β-hCG mandatory in females |
| 12 | Sonar for child and CT tunnel for adult | Ultrasound for children/pregnant; CT gold standard for adults |
| 13 | 3-armed robot with Metro/Cef labels | Laparoscopic appendicectomy; pre-op Metronidazole and Cephalosporin |
| 14 | Flies on a vein and leaking pipe | Complications: Pylephlebitis (portal vein thrombosis) and perforation |
| 15 | Groin stone and kid with runny nose | DDx: Ureteric colic (hematuria) and Mesenteric Adenitis (URTI prodrome) |
